Passage 1: Chinese Mathematicians Make History at Fields Medal

On July 23, 2026, a historic moment occurred at the International Congress of Mathematicians in Philadelphia, USA. Two Chinese mathematicians, Wang Hong and Deng Yu, were awarded the Fields Medal, the most prestigious honor in mathematics. This marks the first time that Chinese citizens have received this distinguished award.

Deng Yu was recognized for his groundbreaking work on partial differential equations. He solved the "narrow formulation" of Hilbert's sixth problem, establishing a complete mathematical logic chain from microscopic particle systems to macroscopic Boltzmann equations. This problem had remained unsolved for 125 years.

Wang Hong received the award for her contributions to harmonic analysis and geometric measure theory. She successfully solved the three-dimensional Kakeya conjecture, a challenge that had puzzled mathematicians for over a century. Her work provides new analytical frameworks for fields such as radar, remote sensing, and signal processing.

Source: CGTN, July 24, 2026

Passage 2: "Study in China" Brand Gains Global Recognition

China's "Study in China" brand is gaining increasing recognition worldwide, according to Education Minister Huai Jinpeng. The minister announced that Chinese language teaching has been incorporated into national education systems in 92 countries, reflecting the growing global interest in Chinese education.

The number of international students studying in China continues to grow rapidly. China is strengthening cooperation with renowned foreign universities in science and engineering fields to attract outstanding overseas youth talent. The goal is to cultivate more individuals who understand and appreciate China.

China's National Smart Education Platform now serves users in over 200 countries and regions. Additionally, Chinese mathematics textbooks have been adopted in British elementary education, and "AI for K12" courses have been promoted to countries including Thailand and Uzbekistan, showcasing China's educational innovation on the global stage.
